Last updated: June 19, 2026

Introduction

HomeCalcs.net (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website located at homecalcs.net. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our site. Please read it carefully.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our calculators. If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name and email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically collected information: When you visit HomeCalcs.net, our servers and third-party analytics tools may automatically collect standard log data including your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and pages visited. This data is used in aggregate to understand how visitors use the site and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Cookies

We participate in the Google AdSense program. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

We use the information we collect to:
