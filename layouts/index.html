Why Use a Mortgage Calculator?

Understanding your mortgage before you commit is one of the most important steps in the home-buying process. A small difference in interest rate or loan term can mean tens of thousands of dollars over the life of your loan.

Our free calculators give you instant, accurate estimates for monthly payments, total interest paid, affordability limits, and refinance savings — with no registration required.

How Mortgage Payments Work

Your monthly mortgage payment is made up of principal (paying down your loan balance) and interest (the cost of borrowing). Most loans also require escrow for property taxes and homeowners insurance, making up your full PITI payment.

The most common mortgage terms are 30-year fixed and 15-year fixed. A 30-year loan has lower monthly payments; a 15-year loan saves significantly on total interest.
